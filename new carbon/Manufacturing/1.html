Why Emission Monitoring Matters

Manufacturing drives global economies but accounts for ~30% of CO₂ emissions. Heavy machinery, process heat, and logistics produce pollutants like CO₂, NOx, PM, and VOCs, impacting air quality and climate.

In India, the CPCB mandates CEMS for 17 high-pollution sectors, including automotive and textiles. Companies like Tata Motors, Nestlé, Arvind Mills, Britannia, and Dixon Tech face regulatory and investor pressure to reduce emissions. ESG frameworks (GRI, BRSR) demand transparent data.

CarbonTally’s solutions enable compliance, energy optimization, and a sustainable reputation.

How CarbonTally Empowers You

Our platform combines IoT sensors, AI analytics, and intuitive dashboards for actionable insights:

  • Track CO₂, NOx, PM, SO₂, and VOCs in real-time from critical equipment.
  • Optimize energy to reduce costs and emissions.
  • Ensure compliance with CPCB, EPA, and global standards.
  • Generate ESG reports for Scope 1, 2, and 3 emissions (GRI, TCFD, BRSR).
  • Predictive maintenance to prevent emission spikes.
  • Tailored solutions for automotive, textiles, electronics, and more.
