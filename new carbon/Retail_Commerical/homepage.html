Emissions Hidden in Storefronts

Retail operations consume significant energy through refrigeration, lighting, HVAC systems, and supply chain logistics. These contribute heavily to Scope 3 emissions, often overlooked but critical to sustainability goals.

From sprawling malls to small franchises, energy inefficiency drives up costs and carbon footprints. CarbonTally’s IoT platform provides real-time insights to optimize energy use and reduce emissions.

How CarbonTally Empowers You

CarbonTally delivers advanced energy monitoring for retail and commercial operations:

  • Track power usage in real-time and convert to CO₂ equivalents.
  • Suggest energy-saving strategies based on time-of-day data.
  • Integrate seamlessly into stores, malls, and warehouses.

Lower costs and emissions with CarbonTally’s data-driven solutions.
